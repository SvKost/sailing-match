How to Play

Swap adjacent items to create rows or columns of three or more identical ones

Match four or more items to create special items with unique effects

Remove obstacles like anchors, ropes, and more by matching nearby items

Complete levels by collecting specific items or achieving score targets

Utilize special boosters to help clear challenging levels

Progress through the map by completing levels and unlocking new challenges

Join special events and challenges for additional rewards

Compare scores and compete with friends by connecting the game to social media
